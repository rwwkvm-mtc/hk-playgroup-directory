About Hong Kong Playgroup Directory

Our Mission

We help parents in Hong Kong find the best early childhood education programs for their children. Our comprehensive directory covers playgroups, pre-nurseries, and kindergartens across all 18 districts.

How to Use Our Platform

  1. Search for institutions by district, name, or specific requirements
  2. Use filters to narrow down your options based on age group, fees, and language
  3. Read detailed information about each institution including facilities and reviews
  4. Submit applications directly through our platform

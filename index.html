About Hong Kong Playgroup Directory

Our Mission

We help parents in Hong Kong find the best early childhood education programs for their children aged 6 months to 3 years. Our comprehensive directory covers playgroups and pre-nursery programs across all 18 districts.

Program Types We Cover

Playgroups

Designed for children aged 6 months to 3 years, playgroups focus on early development through play-based activities, sensory experiences, and social interaction. These programs help develop motor skills, language, and social awareness.

Pre-Nursery Programs

For children aged 2-3 years, pre-nursery programs provide structured learning to prepare children for kindergarten. They focus on academic foundations, language development, and school readiness skills.

How to Use Our Platform

  1. Search for programs by district, name, or specific requirements
  2. Use filters to narrow down your options based on age group, program type, fees, and language
  3. Read detailed information about each program including facilities and reviews
  4. Submit applications directly through our platform
